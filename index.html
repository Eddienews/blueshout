Listen to the Bluesky posts you follow.

Blueshout reads new posts out loud using fast server-side TTS, so you can keep up hands-free. This is an early Beta — we’re improving voices and stability continuously.

  • Log in with a Bluesky App Password (never your main password).
  • Press Start. New posts are queued and read one by one.
  • Transcript is On by default. Tap Transcript to hide it.

Privacy & notes

• Bluesky → Settings → Advanced → App Passwords.
• App Password only — we never ask for your main Bluesky password.
• If audio doesn’t start on iPhone, tap Start again (iOS autoplay rules).
• Some languages may be skipped (e.g., certain CJK posts).
